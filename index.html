Research Interests

My recent research explores the combinatorial and probabilistic properties of Erdős matrices, a class of bistochastic matrices that saturate the Marcus–Ree inequality:

\[\|A\|_{\operatorname{F}}^2\leq \max_{\sigma\in S_n} \sum_{i=1}^{n}A_{i, \sigma(i)}\;,\]

which is satisfied by all $n \times n$ bistochastic matrices.
